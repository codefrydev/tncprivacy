## Commitment

CodeFryDev endeavours to render Digital Properties accessible to the broadest practicable audience, including persons with disabilities, in alignment with principles consistent with **WCAG 2.1** Level AA where reasonably achievable—following practices advocated by the [W3C Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/) and accessibility statements published by major technology platforms.

---

## Measures Undertaken

The Service Provider implements, where feasible:

- semantic HTML markup and ARIA labelling in actively maintained products;
- keyboard navigability across core interaction pathways;
- colour contrast considerations within the PaperMod-based documentation infrastructure;
- responsive layouts accommodating mobile and desktop viewports; and
- incremental improvements as individual products undergo revision.

---

## Known Limitations

Given the heterogeneous, multi-product character of the open source Ecosystem:

- legacy or experimental tools may not fully conform to WCAG standards;
- third-party embeds (analytics platforms, external APIs) may introduce accessibility barriers beyond the Service Provider's direct control;
- games and canvas-based utilities may exhibit limited screen-reader compatibility; and
- community-maintained forks remain the sole responsibility of their respective maintainers.

---

## Feedback and Accommodation Requests

Persons encountering accessibility barriers are invited to correspond with [codefrydev@gmail.com](mailto:codefrydev@gmail.com), subject line **"Accessibility Feedback"**, furnishing:

- the product URL or identifier;
- a description of the barrier encountered; and
- particulars of assistive technology employed, if applicable.

The Service Provider shall endeavour to respond within a commercially reasonable timeframe.
