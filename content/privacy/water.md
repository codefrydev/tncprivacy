## Preamble

The present Privacy Policy governs the **Water App** mobile hydration-reminder application published by **CodeFryDev**. It supplements the [General Privacy Policy](/privacy/general/) and should be read in conjunction with the [Water App Supplementary Terms](/term/water/).

### Dissent and Immediate Cessation

Should the User withhold assent to this Policy, **the User must forthwith uninstall the Application** and discontinue all use.

---

## 1. Categories of Information Collected

### Automatically Collected Data

- device Internet Protocol (IP) address;
- device type, operating system, and application version;
- screens or pages visited, session duration, and interaction timestamps;
- crash logs and performance diagnostics.

### Location Data

The Application may collect **approximate location** (not precise GPS unless explicitly enabled by the User) to support location-aware features, aggregated analytics, and service improvement. See Section 4.

### Voluntarily Furnished Information

Contact particulars or profile information where the User elects to register or initiate support correspondence.

---

## 2. Purposes of Processing

Personal Data is processed for the following purposes:

- operation of hydration reminders and core application functionality;
- performance optimisation and defect remediation;
- aggregated analytics and trend analysis;
- compliance with legal and regulatory obligations; and
- transmission of service notices where permitted by applicable law.

The Service Provider **does not sell** Personal Data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application may integrate third-party analytics or cloud services (including platform-native software development kits from Apple or Google on mobile platforms). Their respective privacy policies apply in addition to the present Policy.

Web properties employ **Google Analytics** and **Microsoft Clarity** as explicated in the [General Privacy Policy](/privacy/general/).

---

## 4. Location Data Processing

Location data may be utilised for:

- personalised reminders and recommendations;
- aggregated, anonymised analytics; and
- occasional transmission to external analytics services for application improvement.

---

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained for the duration of Application use and for a commercially reasonable period thereafter. Deletion may be requested via [codefrydev@gmail.com](mailto:codefrydev@gmail.com). Uninstallation of the Application terminates new data collection.

---

## 6. Children

The Application is not directed at persons under sixteen (16) years of age. The Service Provider invites correspondence should a parent or guardian believe a child has furnished Personal Data.

---

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are applied; however, no information system is entirely impervious to compromise. Security concerns should be reported pursuant to the [Security Policy](/term/security-disclosure/).
