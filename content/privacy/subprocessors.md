## Preamble

CodeFryDev engages third-party **subprocessors** to host, analyse, and deliver Digital Properties. The present list reflects transparency practices adopted by enterprise SaaS providers and open source platforms (GitHub, GitLab, Google Cloud).

The Service Provider **does not sell** Personal Data. Material amendments to this list shall be reflected herein with a revised effective date.

## Objection to Subprocessors

Where a User objects to processing by a designated subprocessor, the available remedy is to **discontinue use** of the affected Digital Properties or correspond with the Service Provider at [codefrydev@gmail.com](mailto:codefrydev@gmail.com).
