Ambit and Substantive Coverage of the Master Instrument#

Drawing upon instruments published by major open source projects (GitHub, Mozilla, Apache) and enterprise SaaS platforms, the Master Terms address:

  • contractual formation, dissent, and immediate cessation where assent is withheld
  • all 85+ products together with analytics instrumentation (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity)
  • MIT / open source dual-layer licensing (source code versus hosted service)
  • acceptable use prescriptions, intellectual property, DMCA compliance, API terms
  • AI output disclaimers and prohibited high-risk deployments
  • prohibition of emergency and safety-critical reliance
  • trade mark and logo usage restrictions
  • security disclosure and community code of conduct
  • grievance officer designation (India), regional privacy rights
  • limitation of liability, indemnification, governing law (India)
